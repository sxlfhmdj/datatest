                                          Emilien • 2 years ago

                                          Instead of @DependsOn you use @Primary on beans used for the conf of the DB1: the bean PlatformTransactionManager, the bean LocalContainerEntityManagerFactoryBean and the bean DataSource . And not use any annotations for the same beans for the conf of the DB2. we are using pretty much the same conf but @Primary is designed for that needs. So with this, to insert in DB1 you can @Transactional without any param (bacuse of the @Primary) and to insert in DB2 you have something like this: @Transactional(value = "myTransactionManagerForDB2")
